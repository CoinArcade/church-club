Bulk SMS Module

Our Bulk SMS Module allows you or your Church to send Bulk SMS to your congregants.

The Module works by allowing you to create Groups where you can configure your SMS and the numbers that belong inside the group, once all is correct you can then either create a schedule for your SMS Message which will allow the Message to Auto Send later or you can send the message right away by using the Send Now Button.


SMS Module Limitations

  • Character Length

    All SMS Messages are currently limited by the 150 standard character length for SMS messages, this means that you currently cannot send SMS messages exceeding 150 Characters

  • SMS Credits

    Although you are given complimentary credits this is just to allow you to get the idea of how our Bulk SMS Module works, after that you are expected to purchase your own Bulk Messages through the Account folder, found right at the top of this page. Purchasing bulk credits allows you to continue using the SMS Module at your own cost.
