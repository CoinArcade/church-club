Church Admin Forums

Church Admins Forums are meant to facilitate open and private communication between churches and members / congregants of those churches there are two kinds of forums available in church admins,

Public Forums

Public Forums are available to all members of church admins, meaning if you create a public forum a member of church admin from another church will be able to participate in the forum and also view all your contributions.

Private Forums

Are available only to the members of your own Church meaning anyone registered within your church can view and participate in the Forums.

Both public and private forums are useful for the following:

as a means of encouraging participation by fellow congregants by allowing them to ask questions on previous church sermons as or if they are made available in the Forums.
